# Welcome to Lox

Your Second Brain is ready.

## What is this?

Lox is your personal knowledge management system. Notes you write here are automatically indexed with AI embeddings, making them searchable by meaning — not just keywords.

## How it works

1. **Write notes** in Obsidian (or any Markdown editor)
2. **Git sync** pushes changes to your VM every 2 minutes
3. **Vault Watcher** detects changes and generates embeddings
4. **MCP Server** lets Claude Code search and write notes

## Folder structure

- **1 - Fleeting Notes/** — Quick captures, ideas, raw thoughts
- **2 - Projects/** — Project hub notes (Maps of Content)
- **2 - Source Material/** — Articles, books, podcasts, videos
- **3 - Tags/** — Tag notes for graph navigation
- **5 - Templates/** — Obsidian templates for new notes
- **6 - Atomic Notes/** — Your main knowledge base (permanent notes)
- **7 - Meeting Notes/** — Meeting captures with attendees and action items

## Quick start

1. Open Obsidian and start writing in `1 - Fleeting Notes/`
2. Ask Claude: *"search my brain for..."*
3. Refine fleeting notes into atomic notes in `6 - Atomic Notes/`
