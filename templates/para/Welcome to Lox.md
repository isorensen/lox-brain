# Welcome to Lox

Your Second Brain is ready.

## What is this?

Lox is your personal knowledge management system using the PARA method. Notes you write here are automatically indexed with AI embeddings, making them searchable by meaning — not just keywords.

## Folder structure (PARA method)

- **1 - Inbox/** — Capture everything here first
- **2 - Projects/** — Active projects with clear outcomes
- **3 - Areas/** — Ongoing areas of responsibility
- **4 - Resources/** — Reference material for future use
- **5 - Archive/** — Completed or inactive items

## Quick start

1. Capture new ideas in `1 - Inbox/`
2. Move them to the appropriate PARA folder
3. Ask Claude: *"search my brain for..."*
